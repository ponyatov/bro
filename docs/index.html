This is a collection of embedded Linux jigs powered by Buildroot build system. This toolset expands the base Buildroot with many packages and additional configs for ARM/MIPS control boards I use, and some software used in my projects.

Some of them are commercial and covered by contracts and NDA, but most parts were publicated for coworking and improvements by a community (if you want to share and contribute).
